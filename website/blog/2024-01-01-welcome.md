Welcome to **CloudCaptain** — your open-source, community-driven learning hub for Cloud, DevOps, AI, and Operations!

<!-- truncate -->

## Our Mission

CloudCaptain was born from a simple idea: learning cloud technologies shouldn't be scattered across dozens of bookmarks and browser tabs. We wanted to create **one place** where anyone — from complete beginners to seasoned professionals — could find structured, high-quality learning resources.

## What Makes CloudCaptain Different?

- **Community-curated** — Every resource has been vetted by practitioners
- **Structured learning paths** — Not just links, but roadmaps
- **Always free** — Open source, forever
- **Interview prep included** — Practical preparation for real interviews
- **Hands-on focused** — Labs, exercises, and projects

## How to Get Involved

1. Star us on [GitHub](https://github.com/nomadicmehul/CloudCaptain)
2. Pick a [learning path](/docs/learning-paths/devops) and start learning
3. Found something great? [Contribute](/contribute) it back!
4. Follow [@nomadicmehul](https://twitter.com/nomadicmehul) for updates

Together, let's navigate the cloud like captains!
