# AWS Interview Preparation

Comprehensive interview preparation guide for Amazon Web Services. We have compiled 60+ commonly asked AWS interview questions with detailed answers, organized by difficulty level.

**[Go to AWS Interview Questions](/docs/cloud/aws/interview-questions)** — Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced questions with architecture examples.

## Key Areas to Study

Before your interview, make sure you are comfortable with these topics:

- **Cloud Fundamentals** — Global infrastructure, pricing, Well-Architected Framework ([Fundamentals](/docs/cloud/aws/fundamentals))
- **Compute & Networking** — EC2, Lambda, VPC, ELB, Route 53 ([Compute & Networking](/docs/cloud/aws/compute-networking))
- **Security** — IAM, KMS, encryption, WAF, GuardDuty ([Security & IAM](/docs/cloud/aws/security-iam))
- **Databases & Storage** — S3, RDS, DynamoDB, Aurora, Redshift ([Databases & Storage](/docs/cloud/aws/databases-storage))
- **DevOps** — CI/CD, CloudFormation, containers, deployment strategies ([DevOps & CI/CD](/docs/cloud/aws/devops-cicd))
- **CLI Commands** — Be fluent with AWS CLI ([Cheat Sheet](/docs/cloud/aws/cheatsheet))
