# Welcome to CloudCaptain

**CloudCaptain** is a dynamic community initiative dedicated to deepening your understanding of cloud technology and its applications. Our mission is to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills they need to become cloud computing experts.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Structured Learning Paths** — Go from beginner to expert with our curated roadmaps
- **40+ Technology Topics** — Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, AWS, Azure, GCP, and much more
- **Interview Prep** — Ace your DevOps and Cloud engineering interviews
- **Hands-on Exercises** — Learn by doing with practical labs and projects
- **Cheatsheets** — Quick reference guides for every tool
- **Community-Curated Resources** — The best articles, videos, and tutorials, voted by the community

## Who Is This For?

Whether you're a **complete beginner** entering the cloud industry or an **experienced professional** seeking to expand your knowledge, CloudCaptain offers something for everyone.

| Your Level | Start Here |
|:-----------|:-----------|
| Complete Beginner | [DevOps Learning Path](/docs/learning-paths/devops) |
| Know the basics | [Containers Learning Path](/docs/learning-paths/containers) |
| Want to go deep | [SRE & Platform Engineering](/docs/learning-paths/sre) |
| Preparing for interviews | [Interview Prep](/docs/interview-prep/devops) |

## How to Contribute

CloudCaptain is **100% open source** and community-driven. Every contribution matters — from fixing a typo to adding an entire learning path.

1. Fork the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/nomadicmehul/CloudCaptain)
2. Add your resources in the appropriate category
3. Submit a Pull Request
4. Join the crew!
